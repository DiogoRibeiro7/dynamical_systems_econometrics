# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background and identity.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Respectful language and constructive feedback.
- Focus on evidence and good reasoning.
- Welcoming others and helping newcomers.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory comments.
- Personal attacks or threats.
- Any behavior that disrupts collaboration.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported by opening an issue with the label `help wanted` and a clear description, or by contacting the project maintainer directly.

Maintainers may remove comments, block users, or close issues/PRs that violate this policy.

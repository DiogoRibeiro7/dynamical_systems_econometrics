# Return Times

## Focus

The return-time layer studies how long a series takes to revisit a stress region after a previous visit.

## Implemented outputs

- inter-event durations
- return-time tables
- rolling or calendar recurrence rates
- empirical survival curves
- regime summaries for return-time distributions

## Limitation

Empirical return times are regime-dependent and often estimated from short samples. They are descriptive recurrence summaries, not exact forecasts of the next crisis date.

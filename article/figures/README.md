## Stable article-facing assets

- `01_conceptual_pipeline.png`: conceptual workflow from raw series to article evidence
- `02_synthetic_extremes_comparison.png`: clustered versus more isolated synthetic extremes
- `03_return_time_distribution.png`: return-time summary for a benchmark observable
- `04_extremal_index_by_series.png`: cross-series clustering comparison
- `05_multivariate_stress_timeline.png`: joint-stress timeline from multivariate synthetic data
- `06_econometric_vs_recurrence_diagnostics.png`: baseline volatility versus event-diagnostic alignment

## What these figures support

- conceptual comparison between clustered and isolated extremes
- descriptive recurrence and return-time summaries
- descriptive multivariate stress timelines

## What they do not support

- causal claims
- literal deterministic-law claims about observed economies
